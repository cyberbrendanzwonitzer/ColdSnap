# Information Architecture

## Primary Navigation Sitemap

| Level 1 Page | Level 2 Sections | Core Goal |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Home | Hero, Benefits, How It Works, Social Proof, Footer CTA | Convert traffic into first booking or lead form completion. |
| Services | Whole Body Cryotherapy, Localized Cryotherapy, Red Light Therapy | Explain service outcomes, mechanism, and session expectations. |
| Pricing | Intro Offers, Memberships, Packages, Add-Ons | Create a transparent purchase path and support recurring revenue. |
| First Timers | FAQ, What to Wear, Safety, Waivers, Contraindications | Reduce anxiety and booking friction for new clients. |
| About and Contact | Team, Mission, Location Map, Hours, Contact Methods | Build trust and local accessibility confidence. |

## Homepage Content Hierarchy

1. Header and Nav
2. Hero (value proposition and primary CTA)
3. Three Pillars (Recover, Revive, Relieve)
4. Interactive Chamber Safety Block
5. Social Proof Strip
6. Mid-page conversion CTA (First Freeze Special)
7. Footer with contact, social links, and newsletter

## Conversion Paths

1. Immediate booking from hero CTA
2. Learn-more route through Services before booking
3. First-timer route through FAQ and safety content
4. Lead-capture route through newsletter or promotional CTA
